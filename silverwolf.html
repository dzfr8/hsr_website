Overview

Faction: Stellaron Hunters

Silver Wolf is a genius hacker and a key member of the Stellaron Hunters. She is known for tactical precision and works excellently with Acheron and Black Swan in team synergies.

Role

Silver Wolf generally functions as a support and damage dealer with great utility in combat scenarios focusing on debuffs and energy regeneration.
