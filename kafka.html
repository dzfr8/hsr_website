Overview

Faction: Stellaron Hunters

Path: Nihility (Lightning)

Role: DPS

Abilities

  • Attack - Midnight Tumult: Deals minor Lightning damage to a single enemy.
  • Skill - Caressing Moonlight: Deals Lightning damage to multiple enemies; if targets have Damage Over Time (DoT), additional damage is applied.
  • Ultimate - Twilight Trill: Deals AoE Lightning damage and can Shock enemies; Shocked enemies take additional damage over time.
  • Talent - Gentle but Cruel: After an ally uses a basic attack, Kafka follows up with a Lightning attack that can Shock the enemy.

Lore

Kafka is a composed, beautiful, and deadly agent of the Stellaron Hunters. She uses the enchantment of Spirit Whisper to support allies and absorb Stellaron energy. Her elegance conceals a potent power that devastates foes with precision lightning attacks. Her personal hobby includes shopping and organizing her collection of coats.
